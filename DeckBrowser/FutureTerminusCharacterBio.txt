The futuristic Highwind City's alternate version of Terminus had long since given up on the HCPD, tiring of deferring to corporate interests and local politics. Besides, the police were largely focused on supervillains and rogue AI, not the elusive specters that had cost her an arm, an eye, and a trip to the psych unit. 

She decided to deal with the ghostly threats herself, using powers she had gained from the encounters. Her first target: the demonic red figure that had stolen her arm, and now seemed to be after the rest of her.
