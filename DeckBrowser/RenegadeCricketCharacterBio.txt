Framed and arrested as a result of the villainous Python's hypnosis, Kate Shreeve was left stranded on the wrong side of the law. Her mentor Echelon was among the few who knew the truth, and the information broker sent a team of willing heroes to help Shreeve escape and go on the run. 

Now Cricket is determined to clear her name and bring Python to justice, even if it means going undercover as a criminal.
