After almost killing herself by traveling to the years 2199 and 1929, Drift began searching for a safer way to time travel. Her powers were better suited to guiding her through the time streams than actually moving her, so she sought out the temporal anomaly which served as prison to the enigmatic Mistress of Fate. 

From within the anomaly, Drift was able to open doorways to various points throughout history. Now all she needed was to find the right door...
