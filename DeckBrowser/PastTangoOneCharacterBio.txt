After her reform from mercenary to hero, Tango One found that there were all kinds of benefits to working with other gifted individuals - particularly a teleporter like Vanish. Instantly being transported to an ideal sniper's nest beat climbing up the old fashioned way, and there was no better spotter. 

But there were also downsides, like being present when Vanish's gadgets broke and stranded their whole team in the 1920s...
