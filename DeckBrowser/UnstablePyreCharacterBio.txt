As Pyre, Gray found found some satisfaction in assisting his former enemies, helping them deal with greater threats. But despite all his efforts to finally stabilize his physiology, the knowledge that his true power was locked away inside his containment suit was maddening. 

He knew that if he simply unlocked it, he could burn away his foes in mere moments. His new associates, for reasons both altruistic and practical, helped him keep control, but he knew one day the situation would require him to breach containment.
