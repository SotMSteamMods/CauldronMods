While protecting Windmill City from an invading alien poacher, Gyrosaur encountered several agents of the Nightlore Council, and was surprised to learn she had a long-lost aunt named Talin Brosk serving aboard their Citadel in the far reaches of space. 

Observing Gyrosaur's speed, Brosk convinced her to go undercover in an illegal intergalactic racing circuit, to help take it down from the inside...
