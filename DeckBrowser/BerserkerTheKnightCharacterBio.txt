Almost immediately after his awakening in the 21st Century, Sir George was thrust into combat with a foe he thought long-vanquished: the dragon Tiamat. She had apparently recovered from her decapitation centuries before and was wreaking havoc across the land. Other strange warriors showed up to help the knight: a sun-god, a vanishing woman, and even a necromancer. 

They met with little success; the dragon's elemental magic had grown even stronger since its resurrection. As the Knight's armor broke and his shield shattered, he determined that such ferocity could only be matched by an equally primal rage. So he took a broken sword in each hand, gave a tremendous battle cry, and charged...
