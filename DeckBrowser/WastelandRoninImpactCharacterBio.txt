During his time as a renegade hero, Impact had formed an unlikely bond with Teryx, the Wandering Isle, a massive and ancient turtle that sheltered him and his companions. Months later, rejoining Cricket after her name had been cleared and her secret identity restored, another misadventure left them stranded in a post-apocalyptic future.

Fleeing from hordes of monsters and in need of someplace safe to regroup, Impact somehow found the still-living Teryx, crawling across the parched seabed. He approached the turtle, hoping that something in it might still recognize him...
