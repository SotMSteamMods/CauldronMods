In recent years something had seemed to draw every alien warlord, spacefaring mercenary, or reality-altering superbeing toward the Earth, and Starlight had been instrumental in safeguarding the planet. Despite being one of the Nightlore Council's newest agents, Starlight's experience soon surpassed most of the senior councilors. 

As she proved herself ever more capable, she began relinquishing her sector duties in order to lend her experience on a broader scale, training fresh Nightlore agents and coordinating Council operations. Her power and wisdom earned her, and her planet, a reputation that reached the farthest corners of the galaxy - but not all of the attention she drew was favorable.
