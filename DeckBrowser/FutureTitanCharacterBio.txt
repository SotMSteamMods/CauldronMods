In an alternate 2199, special agent Roland White of Highwind City used his strange powers (and a large-caliber revolver) to bring down superpowered threats. 

His reckless streak frequently got him into trouble, and though his demonic alter ego was made of nigh-indestructible obsidian, it still cracked under high-velocity armor-piercing rounds...such as those used by the HCPD gunships that kept a suspiciously close eye on him.
